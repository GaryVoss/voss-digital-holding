Looking for Satellite Creative?

After 25 successful years in the industry, Satellite Creative Ltd has now ceased trading.

While Satellite’s doors have closed, the people who made it what it was are continuing to do what they do best.

VossDigital - Web Development & Digital Support

Voss Digital Ltd is a Newcastle upon Tyne–based web development agency providing professional website development, ongoing maintenance, and technical support to businesses throughout the UK.

Founded in 2025 by Gary Voss, who previously served as Lead Developer at Satellite Creative, VossDigital continues to support many of Satellite’s long-standing clients while building new partnerships with businesses seeking reliable, professional web development services.

Services include:

  • Bespoke website development (Craft CMS, WordPress)
  • Monthly website maintenance packages
  • Web application development
  • Technical support and consultation
  • Hosting management
