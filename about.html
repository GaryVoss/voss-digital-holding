About VossDigital

Voss Digital Ltd is a Newcastle upon Tyne based web development agency providing professional website development, ongoing maintenance, and technical support to businesses throughout the UK.

Founded in 2025 by Gary Voss, following the closure of Satellite Creative, VossDigital continues to serve many of Satellite's former clients while building new partnerships with businesses seeking reliable, professional web development services.

Services

  • Bespoke website development (Craft CMS, WordPress)
  • Monthly website maintenance packages
  • Web application development
  • Technical support and consultation
  • Hosting management
